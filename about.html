Sustainability

Sustainability is a word that we increasingly come across in all aspects of life. We probably all have an overall understanding that it is about the avoidance of the depletion of natural resources in order to maintain an ecological balance. In business, we may see it more specifically as the idea that goods and services should be produced in ways that do not use resources that cannot be replaced and that do not damage the environment. Sustainability is a broad subject and can be broken down into many different sections that all affect our lives on a day to day basis. We can positively contribute to each of these areas to help sustain the world for future generations. By recycling, we can make a remarkable difference to our planet.

About Us

Our idea was to train an algorithm so that it is able to recognise different kinds of materials by image processing. This algorithm is then able to sort waste into 6 different categories. Essentially, this is supposed to be part of the software for a waste recycling machine since many people do not recycle their waste correctly which has a huge negative environmental impact.
